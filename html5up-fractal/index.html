LAUNFE

Your friendly neighbourhood laundry store, with an exclusive cafe for taking rest.

Designed based on Fractal in html5up, completed by Mao.

In Store Café

You can take a rest in our cafe while you are waiting for your clothes to be cleaned.

Laundry Service

We provide self-service laundry and reception for receiving your order so that you do not have to do your laundry by yourself.

Delivery Service

We provide delivery service, if you do not want to waste your time, we will deliver your laundry to your house in anytime you would like to collect. We can also collect your clothes from your home to our store for laundry service.
